Q: How is WPM calculated?
A: WPM (Words Per Minute) is calculated as (correct characters typed ÷ 5) ÷ minutes. One word is standardized as 5 characters.

Q: How is accuracy measured?
A: Accuracy = (correct characters ÷ total characters typed) × 100%. Only matching characters count as correct.

Q: Can I practice in both languages?
A: Yes! Switch between English and Korean using the language selector. Each language has its own set of practice sentences.
